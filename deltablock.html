The Delta Block at VIT Chennai is a four-storey academic building, dedicated to the School of Computing Science and Engineering. It houses modern facilities including spacious classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, and high-speed internet connectivity. The building is designed to facilitate interactive learning, with comfortable seating arrangements and modern audio-visual equipment. The delta block houses automation and robotics laboratories that are equipped with the latest hardware and software. The ground floor has a spacious lobby for students to interact with their peers and faculty members. The Delta Block offers a conducive learning environment for students pursuing computer science and engineering programs at VIT Chennai.
