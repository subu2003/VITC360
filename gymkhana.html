The Gymkhana at VIT Chennai is a student-run organization that provides opportunities for students to explore their interests and engage in extracurricular activities. It offers a range of student clubs, including technical, cultural, sports, and social service organizations. These clubs organize various events and activities throughout the academic year, including competitions, performances, and community service initiatives. The Gymkhana promotes student welfare, leadership, and overall development, and is an integral part of the VIT community. The Gymkhana also has restaurants including dominoes pizza and tea tier. The AC gym situated at the first floor enables students to engage in physical fitness and strength training. Other sports like Squash, Table Tennis, Snooker and Rock Climbing provide a relaxation to students from their daily routine.
